Privacy Policy

Seol — Irish Waters Sailing Log

Last updated: 28 March 2026

Overview

Seol is designed with privacy in mind. Your sailing data stays on your device and, if you choose to enable iCloud, syncs privately through Apple's iCloud infrastructure. We do not operate servers that store your personal data, and we do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

Data You Enter

Seol stores the following information that you provide:

  • Vessel details (name, type, registration, call sign, MMSI)
  • Passage plans and records (ports, dates, distances, fuel use)
  • Ship's log entries (timestamps and free text)
  • Crew member names
  • Waypoints and routes
  • Sail inventory
  • Weather observations
  • Tidal information
  • Pilotage notes

This data is stored locally on your device using Core Data. If you are signed in to iCloud, it is also synced to your private iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit service, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Location Data

When a passage is underway, Seol requests access to your device's GPS to record your position. Location data is used to:

  • Log GPS track points in your passage record
  • Generate automatic position log entries
  • Display your position on the passage map

Location data is stored only on your device (and in your private iCloud account if sync is enabled). It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

You can revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings → Seol → Location.

Third-Party Services

Marine Institute Ireland (ERDDAP) — Seol fetches tidal prediction data from the Irish Marine Institute's public ERDDAP service. Requests include a date range and station identifier; no personal data is sent. Data is provided under CC BY 4.0.

WorldTides — For ports not covered by Marine Institute gauges, Seol may request tidal predictions from the WorldTides API using a GPS coordinate. No personal data is included in these requests. See WorldTides Privacy Policy.

Met Éireann — Seol fetches sea area weather forecasts from Met Éireann's public API. No personal data is sent.

Analytics & Advertising

Seol contains no analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or crash reporting services. No usage data is collected or transmitted.

Children's Privacy

Seol is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
