Seol

Irish Waters Sailing Log & Passage Planner

About

Seol is a native iOS app for planning and recording sailing passages in Irish waters. Keep a proper ship's log, track tidal information, manage your crew and sail inventory, and build a record of every passage.

Your data is stored on your device and synced privately via iCloud — it is never shared with third parties.

Features

  • Passage planning with departure and arrival ports
  • Live tidal predictions for Irish Marine Institute gauge stations
  • Secondary port tidal data via WorldTides
  • Automatic GPS track logging while underway
  • Ship's log with timestamped entries
  • Weather observations and VHF radio log
  • Crew and sail inventory management
  • Pilotage guides for harbour approaches
  • iCloud sync across your devices

Privacy

Seol collects only the data you enter and the GPS positions recorded during your passages. All data is stored on your device and synced via iCloud. No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising.
